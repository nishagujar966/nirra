About Us

Welcome to The Padmavathy Nilayam Guest House*, a serene haven nestled in the divine hills of Tirumala. We offer a peaceful and comfortable stay for pilgrims and travelers seeking rest amidst the sacred surroundings of Lord Venkateswara’s abode.

Our rest house is designed to blend traditional warmth with modern comfort, ensuring every guest experiences both tranquility and convenience. Located close to major temples and attractions, Padmavathy Nilayam Guest House provides easy access while maintaining a calm and spiritual atmosphere.

Whether you’re here for a short visit or a spiritual retreat, our clean rooms, courteous service, and homely ambiance make your stay memorable. At The Ram Bagicha Guest House, we believe in offering more than accommodation — we offer an experience of devotion, peace, and comfort.
